## Breakpoints

|                   | xsmall | small | medium | large  | xlarge |
|-------------------|--------|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| Alias             | xs     | sm    | md     | lg     | xl     |
| Minimum width     | 320px  | 600px | 768px  | 1024px | 1440px |
| Columns           | 4      | 8     | 8      | 12     | 12     |
| Margins / Gutters | 16px   | 16px  | 24px   | 24px   | 24px   |
